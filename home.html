Airfare and Aviation Gasoline Prices
Having spent a significant amount of time researching investment strategies involving airlines and dealing with the macroeconomic data of the air travel industry, it suprises me how often claims are made about air travel that are not immediately backed up with data. A piece from the New York Times Editorial Board claimed, amoung other things, that the cost of fuel is not a factor in pricing airfare. They are correct, but this is not an obvious fact nd they provided no evidence for it. I wrote up a quick paper that goes about proving the price of avaiation gasoline and airfair are independent and the accomanying R code.
